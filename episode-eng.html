The 'BUT Informatique for Beginners' series entertainingly presents the six skills of the BUT Informatique degree.

Season

1

1. Realize

2 min

In the first episode, you embark on the discovery of the 'Realize' skill: you will learn how to develop applications and adapt them to any type of platform based on the client's requirements and needs.

2. Optimize

2 min

This second episode will delve into the 'Optimize' skill: you will learn how to analyze and optimize applications by selecting the most suitable algorithms to best address given problems.

3. Administration

2 min

In this episode, we will unveil the backstage of the 'Administration' skill. You will understand the meaning of installing and configuring a workstation and deploying services within a network architecture.

4. Manage

2 min

In the episode 'Manage', you will delve into the design, implementation, and administration of a database that meets the client's needs.

5. Lead

2 min

The episode 'Lead' highlights teamwork and the importance of identifying business needs for clients and users, emphasizing the application of project management methodologies.

6. Collaborate

2 min

Finally, in the last episode 'Collaborate', you will explore the relevance of identifying personal skills and emphasize the importance of defining one's role and responsibilities within a computer science team.
